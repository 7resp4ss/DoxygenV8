◆ stack_support

StackSupport cppgc::Heap::HeapOptions::stack_support = StackSupport::kSupportsConservativeStackScan

Specifies whether conservative stack scan is supported.

When conservative stack scan is not supported, the collector may try to invoke garbage collections using non-nestable task, which are guaranteed to have no interesting stack, through the provided Platform. If such tasks are not supported by the Platform, the embedder must take care of invoking the GC through ForceGarbageCollectionSlow().
