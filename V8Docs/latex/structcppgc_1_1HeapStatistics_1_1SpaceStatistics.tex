\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Space granularity statistics. For each space the statistics record the space name, the amount of allocated memory and overall used memory for the space. The statistics also contain statistics for each of the space\textquotesingle{}s pages, its freelist and the objects allocated on the space.
