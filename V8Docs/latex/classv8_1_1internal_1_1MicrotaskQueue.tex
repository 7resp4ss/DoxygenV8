Adds a callback to notify the embedder after microtasks were run. The callback is triggered by explicit Run\+Microtasks call or automatic microtasks execution (see \mbox{\hyperlink{classv8_1_1Isolate_a6bc2069413d88ad90e4c8bee0b9e4037}{Isolate\+::\+Set\+Microtasks\+Policy}}).

Callback will trigger even if microtasks were attempted to run, but the microtasks queue was empty and no single microtask was actually executed.

Executing scripts inside the callback will not re-\/trigger microtasks and the callback.
