\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Helper class to create a snapshot data blob. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classv8_1_1Isolate}{Isolate}} used by a \mbox{\hyperlink{classv8_1_1SnapshotCreator}{Snapshot\+Creator}} is owned by it, and will be entered and exited by the constructor and destructor, respectively; The destructor will also destroy the \mbox{\hyperlink{classv8_1_1Isolate}{Isolate}}. Experimental language features, including those available by default, are not available while creating a snapshot.
